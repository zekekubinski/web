A Letter From Out Executive Director

Dear Trips for Kids Bay Area Community,

Our community has been critical to our stability and success over the last year. Trips for Kids Bay Area would not be where it is today without all of you - thank you!

From corporations, foundations, family advised funds and individuals who joined our donor community, to folks who donated bikes, clothes, or volunteered on a ride with kids or at a biking event - our community keeps showing up and we are grateful.

Many thanks for your steadfast support.

Warmly,

Dr. Louise Malandra

Thanks for a Great Family MTB Festival

What an amazing time at our Family MTB Festival this past weekend! It was a perfect day to spend riding along the bay and relaxing in Pickleweed Park eating pizza! Huge thank you to all of our Staff, Interns, volunteers, and donors who make community events like this possible. Our goal of getting kids on bikes to further outdoor equity and community engagement in underserved communities is possible one step at a time through events like these.
